Short Bio

Chongsheng Zhang (Senior Member, IEEE) received the Ph.D. degree from INRIA, France. Since then, he has been working with Henan University, China, where he is a Professor and a Leader of the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Team.

He has published 60 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences, including AAAI and KDD. He has authored eight books and filed 20 Chinese patents.

His research interests include imbalance learning, OCR, and digital paleography.
